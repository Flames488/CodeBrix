1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how CodeBrix ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (codebrixweb.com). By using our website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience by:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Understanding how you use our website
  • Providing personalized content
  • Improving our services

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be switched off.

3.2 Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously.

3.3 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable the website to provide enhanced functionality and personalization.

3.4 Targeting Cookies

These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners to build a profile of your interests.
